Tabs

Hotels

Each tab is 28px height and the width will vary according to the content. The tab will have 10px horizontal padding to each side of the text. The bottom line is 4px height and the width may vary depending on the page layout.

Details/Overlay Page

The tabs for the Hotels Path will appear in the the Details/Overlay Page.

The default tabs are in black and the selected or hightlighted section is in magenta color #ec008e.
There's no mouse-over behavior or underline for the links in the tabs.

MVT Hotels

For MVT Hotels this design is currently under testing. Under the Rooms and Details tab the photo gallery, hotel details, hotel policies and the rooms table will be shown. Under the Maps tab only the Map with the hotel marker will be shown (and the landmarks near the hotel). Finally, under the Customer Reviews tab the TripAdvisor overall rating and the customer reviews will be shown.
