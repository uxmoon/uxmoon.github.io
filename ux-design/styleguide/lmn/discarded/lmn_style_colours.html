colour palette

The lastminute.com colour palette consists of lastminute.com magenta, black and white. No other colours should be used. The colours can be used in their solid form or as tints. The tints help expand the colour palette into a larger range of more expressive colours. When coloured using lastminute.com Magenta, the lastminute.com logotype should always be solid, never a tint.
