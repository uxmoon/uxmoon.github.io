Hotel Cards

Design Breakdown

The hotel card has a title and a description. The title area has a black background #000, the min-height is 30 pixels and the min-width is 550 pixels in the Search Results page. The hotel name font is bold and white #fff, the font-size is 14 pixels and is left aligned. The hotel name is a link and is not underlined by default only on mouseover the underline will appear. The Top Secret Hotel title has the same properties as the hotel name. The "what is this?" link for TSH hotel cards has a font-size of 12 pixels, underlined and a regular weight.

The inner padding is 10 pixels and the height depends on the content. The hotel thumbnail image has a width of 130 pixels and the height is 98 pixels.

The border around the hotel card is 1 pixel solid and the hex color is #CCC. The top and bottom border around the default room name are 1 pixel dashed and the hex color is #CCC.
