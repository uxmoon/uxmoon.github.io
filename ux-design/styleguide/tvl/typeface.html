The typeface of the Travelocity brand is Benton Sans. It reflects the modern and open nature of the brand with clarity and style. No other typefaces should be used inTravelocity collateral. Only the Light and Regular weights of the typeface should be used. Italicised text must not be used. Benton Sans must never be artificially extended, condensed ormanipulated in any way.

Online, the Benton Sans typeface can be used for headings using the sIFR method (or Scalable Inman Flash Replacement).
