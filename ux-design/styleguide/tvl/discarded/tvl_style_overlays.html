Overlays

Fig 1. shows the overlay design on an on hover state.

Design notes:

  • 260px wide
  • Heading should be 12px, bold, left aligned, pink (#ec008c) & lowercase
  • Close link (when needed) should be 12px, black (#000000), underlined & followed by a black cross icon.
  • Main content section will vary in height. The Customer Experience team should be consulted on any new overlays and will deterime whether on not a scroll bar is needed (See Fig 3).

Fig 2. shows the overlay design on an on click state. (addition of the close link & icon.)

Fig 3. shows the overlay design with a scroll bar. This should be used when there's a lot of content to include. The Customer Experience team should be consulted on its use.
